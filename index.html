Alt For Sam

Generate alt text from your Clue By Sam scores that will read better to assistive technology.

Generate alt text

How to use

Once you complete your CluesBySam game, copy the text result and paste it into the box above

Then, clicking the "Copy alt to clipboard" button will copy nicer formatted alt text to your clipboard

To share your results on social media, post a screenshot with the copied text set as "alt text"

Why do this?

Pasting your emoji results into a post and firing it into the world will be a rough experience for assistive technology such as screenreaders.

For example a result could read "Green square, Yellow square, Green square" and so on for all twenty choices.

This website simply compiles your results and generates a prettier string of text that will be friendlier to screenreaders!

For example we'll convert it to "Four clues used, two mistakes made"

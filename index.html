How to use

  • Once you complete your Clues By Sam game, copy the result and paste it into the field above.
  • We'll inject an accessible summary of your results into the text content.
  • You will see a live preview of the generated alt text in the preview field. Verify you are happy with the changes here.
  • Then, click the "Copy alt to clipboard" button to copy this formatted text.

Why do this?

Pasting your emoji results directly into a post will create friction for users of assistive technology, such as screenreaders.

For example a result could read "Green square, Yellow square, Green square" and so on for all twenty choices.

This website simply compiles your results and generates a shorter string of text that will be read out nicely!

For example we'll convert your almost-perfect score to "19 correct guesses. 1 hint".
